Shortly About

We have created a solid platform for our students to showcase their musical talents. Nadasandhva, which is a concert comprising various musical performances, provides students a great opportunity to perform vocal, light vocal, musical instrument playing and Jugalbandhi (a duet performance of two solo musicians of two separate genres) on classical, folk and modern music forms. We also have Kalasandhya celebrations which encourage students to grow and learn through musical performances.
