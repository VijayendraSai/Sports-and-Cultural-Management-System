Welcome to Sahaya Club

In case you wonder:"I don't think I have any special skills....how can I make a difference?"....

By volunteering some time, each of us, including YOU, can make a big difference on the lives of others! The inner satisfaction and richness gained from this process is far beyond your expectations.

We always welcome volunteers, and are always looking for new ideas and suggestions, including new ideas for fundraising projects! You too can make an incredible difference in the life of so many children, who are so appreciative of our efforts to help.

Sahaya Goal

To conduct atleast a blood donation and a visit to any orphanage once in a semester and to spread social awareness among the students .

Sahaya Motto

“The ocean would be less even if a single drop is missing” Likewise,we may not change the world or we may not be able help all the needy.But we can atleast be a helping hand without disturbing our regular busy lives.

Events Organized

Making use of waste papers,old newspapers

All the unused papers from all the hostels and staff quarters are collected. The money obtained by this process is used for donations to orphanages

Blood donation camps

Blood donation camps were conducted in the college campus, with the help of Coimbatore and Pollachi Government hospital, in which a lot of students actively participated and donated blood. This is effectively used in the hospitals whenever there is a need.
